What is the Virtual Voyage to Gaza?

The Virtual Voyage to Gaza tracks ships in a digital space (Sailaway 3) to commemorate and raise awareness about efforts challenging the blockade on Gaza. Since 2007, Palestinians have faced an illegal blockade. online or in real life, this project shows they are not alone. We sail together for a free Palestine.

Inspired by actual missions such as:

  • Freedom Flotilla Coalition (FFC)
  • Thousand Madleens to Gaza (TMTG)
  • Global Sumud Flotilla

The Virtual Voyage To Gaza was created to echo the mission of real-world flotillas sailing towards Gaza. When physical Freedom Flotilla missions face delays or are stopped, the digital world continues to sail in solidarity, keeping their spirit alive online.

Whenever a real flotilla mission fails or is intercepted, we reset our virtual voyage and set sail again, aligning our start with the next launch. This ensures the world remains aware of the ongoing struggle to break the siege on Gaza.

Learn more about the real missions at the Freedom Flotilla Coalition.
